Pocket Orchestra Series

Original Low Brass Music Made For Student Engagement

Created by Isaac Davanzo, the Pocket Orchestra Series is designed to help develop low brass players through music that feels musically rewarding.

We are pioneering a more modern approach to low brass learning that blends thoughtful pedagogy with relatable musical writing. Utilizing backing tracks keeps practice interesting and consistent. Pocket Orchestra is an educational experience that is built for the home.

This series is being developed with the student experience as the top priority and is intended to inspire young musicians to think artistically about how to play low brass.

What makes this series different?

We Leverage Technology To Make Our Pieces Accessible And Enjoyable

The Pocket Orchestra Series is not just a library of PDFs. It is a growing catalog of educational products created to make independent practice, lesson work, and musical development feel more engaging and complete.

Each composition is built to give players an environment that the can apply fundamental skills and develop musicality. We write around unique dynamic themes that always carry a story and challenge the student to think about what they are communicating through the melodies they play on their instrument.
