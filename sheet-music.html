Pocket Orchestra Series

Original low brass music built around a modern approach to student development.

Created by Isaac Davanzo, the Pocket Orchestra Series is designed to help develop low brass players through music that feels current, playable, and musically rewarding.

Rather than treating educational repertoire as something dry or purely functional, Isaac Davanzo is pioneering a more modern approach to low brass learning — one that blends thoughtful pedagogy with fresh musical writing, practical usefulness, and a stronger sense of artistic identity.

These products are intended for players, teachers, and studios looking for music that supports growth while still feeling polished enough to be exciting to rehearse, study, and perform.

What makes this series different

Product-focused materials created by Isaac Davanzo for modern low brass study.

The Pocket Orchestra Series is not just a library of PDFs. It is a growing catalog of educational products created to make independent practice, lesson work, and musical development feel more engaging and more complete.

Each release is built to give players useful materials they can immediately work with, while also reflecting the larger creative vision developed by Isaac Davanzo. The goal is simple: music that helps students improve, but still feels artistically intentional.
