Questions families ask

Helpful Answers Before Booking.

Who are these lessons best for?

These lessons are especially well suited for middle school and high school low brass students who want engaging and high-quality musical experiences.

What does a student typically work on in lessons?

Lessons include targeted development of school repertoire, solo music, audition preparation, and foundational skills and materials.

Are online lessons still effective?

Yes. For many students, online lessons provide a collaborative and safe environment in which they can develop their playing more comfortably. We prioritize this for accessibility and flexibility without sacrificing quality.

Is buying sheet music necessary?

We do not require lesson families to purchase our Pocket Orchestra pieces, but strongly suggest utilizing them to receive the highest quality educational experience. We offer a $5 monthly subscription that allows access to all sheet music resources for all students enrolled in lessons. Otherwise, we ask that families purchase each piece their student is working on.
