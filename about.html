Why a multi-instructor studio helps

More instructors mean better fit and long-term support.

One of the biggest strengths of Low Brass Lessons is that the studio is not limited to a single teaching schedule or a single pedagogical approach. Our broader instructor network makes it easier to match students with teachers who are a strong fit for their instrument, learning style, schedule, and goals.

It also makes the studio more flexible for families. More instructors can mean more availability, more consistency over time, and more pathways for students to stay supported as needs change.

1

Multiple instructor options provides the most flexible model for lesson scheduling. This allows consistent learning even with busy schedules.

2

Families have access to multiple teaching philosophies, so students can best match their learning style to their instructor.

3

A high standard for instructor quality means that families can have peace of mind regarding lesson effectiveness and safety.

Instructor standards

Instructors are selected with clear standards in mind.

Low Brass Lessons is built around the idea that families should feel confident not only in the lesson format, but also in the qualifications and professionalism of the instructors they encounter.

Educational Preparation

Instructors are selected for strong musical and educational backgrounds, including standards such as formal music training and, where applicable, music education degree preparation or completion.

Student-Facing Professionalism

Instructors should be able to communicate clearly, teach responsibly, and represent the studio in a way that is organized, encouraging, and dependable for families.

Safety and Trust

Student safety matters. We place an importance on requirements such as a valid fingerprint clearance cards when considering musicians for working directly with our young musicians and families.

Instructional Fit

Our instructors are able to demonstrate the ability to teach developing musicians patiently, clearly, and with attention to long-term growth. We ensure that every instructor has a clear pedagogical approach.

Lesson formats

In-person Lessons

A strong fit for students who benefit from the energy of being in the room. We account for instructor travel and include an additional $5 charge per lesson paid directly to the instructor to account for travel costs.

What families can expect

A lesson experience designed to feel clear, welcoming, and worthwhile.

Lessons Week To Week

Our model thrives on families that are looking for a long term educational plan for their students growth. We offer free 30 minute consultations in which families can Meet the Instructor via video call to learn more about their process, work through schedules, and get an overall feel for instructor compatibility. This allows families to make a confident and informed decision before committing to lessons.

Often times students enroll in weekly or biweekly lessons to see linear results throughout the year. And, we offer the option to schedule one off lessons with instructors if you'd like to expand the pedagogical perspectives your student is exposed to and maximize their growth. Fundamentally, the students drive and passion is the factor for how much a student can learn, not an instructors availability.

Thoughtful Instruction

Lessons are meant to support progress over time, not just deliver occasional tips. Students should leave with direction, encouragement, and a stronger sense of what growth looks like.

Flexibility Without Losing Quality

Online and in-person options make the studio more accessible and accommodating. Lesson content and quality of instruction remains consistent between each lesson format, making them seamlessly interchangeable.

A Studio That Grows With You

As goals become more advanced, your students will benefit more from a studio built around having long term structure and support for student growth.
